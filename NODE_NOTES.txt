Stateful Authentication :

Imagine this story
You go to a school 
Step 1:
Teacher asks:“Are you a student?”
You say:“Yes”

Step 2:
Teacher gives you a badge/card 
This card = session ID

Step 3:
Next time you come:
You just show the card:“See, I am already a student”
Teacher remembers you 

Same thing in computer:
-User will login
-Server gives ID (session) 
-Browser saves that ID 
Next time:
-Browser sends that ID 
Server says:"yes, this user already login”
